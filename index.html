CHloe Peters

About Chloe

Chloe is a freshman student at Bob Jones University in Greenville, South Carolina. She is majoring in International Studies with a double minor in foreign language and psychology.

Chloe is originally from New Hampshire where she was homeschooled through all of grade school. She has three siblings: an older brother, older sister, and younger sister.

As a child, Chloe learned to play violin, raise animals, compete in public speaking and debate, and host events.

Work Experience

Chloe has worked for her family's HVAC business in New Hampshire as a clerical assistant for five years. She spends her summers in Virginia where she works as a respite care attendant for a child with special needs. While on campus, Chloe works as a Custodial Agent and a Student Ambassador for Alumni Relations.
